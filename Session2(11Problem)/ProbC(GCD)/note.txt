Explain of the case:
This case is tell about GCD (Greatest Common Divisor) where there have 2 input number example
divisors of:
24 and 36
the greatest divisors of the two number is 12 (from 2 * 2 * 3 = 12)

or we can search from listing the factor like:
  24
-------  
1 | 24
2 | 12
3 | 6
4 | 6

  36
-------  
1 | 36
2 | 18
3 | 12
4 | 9
6 | 6

24 = 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12(biggest & same), 24
36 = 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 9, 12(biggest & same), 18, 36

so the greatest common divisor is 12
